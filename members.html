Forms and Memorizations

White Belt Memorization

  1. Say "Good Night" to my parents before I go to bed
  2. Say "good Bye" to my parents whenever I go to school or Taekwondo.
  3. Greet my parents when I return home from school or Taekwondo
  4. Say "Hello, how are you? whenever I meet an elder

Yellow Belt Memorization

  1. Say "Good Night", to my parents before I go to bed
  2. Get up early every morning
  3. Brush your teeth every morning and evening
  4. Obey your parents
  5. Say "Hello, how are you?" whenever you meet an elder

Orange Belt Memorization

  1. The Person that Grand Master Likes:
  2. An honest and disciplined person
  3. A confident person
  4. The person that the Grand Master dislikes:
  5. A person who does not keep his/her promise
  6. A person who lies
  7. A person who makes others do his/her work

Green Belt Memorization

  1. Listen to and obey your parents
  2. Be polite and speak respectfully to your parents
  3. Greet your parents every morning and evening
  4. Maintain a healthy body to prevent any injuries
  5. Speak with your parents frequently to make them happy
  6. Help your parents with their household chores
  7. Help your brothers and sisters and maintain a good relationship with them

Blue Belt Memorizations

  1. To have a sound mind
  2. To maintain a healthy body
  3. To train in self defense and cultivate the mind and spirit
  4. To grow my patiencs and fighting spirit
  5. Believe that I can become the best

Brown Belt Memorization

  1. Etiquette: the conduct and decorum that humans need to protect
  2. Patience: being able to face all kinds of hardships
  3. Sense of Honor: always keeping integrity and knowing when to be shameful
  4. Self Control: being able to overcome self-indulgence wisely
  5. Indomitability: maintaining a strong mentality despite many challenges

Red Belt Memorization

  1. Speaking on the phone
  2. Always say "Thank you" and give your name when answering the phone
  3. Alwayse greet the person when making a call, then give your name and simply state your request
  4. Ways to practice filial piety
  5. Give your mother a message when she is tired
  6. Clean and polish your father's shoes
  7. Help carry out your parents' errands
